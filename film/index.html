About Miyazaki

Born in 1941 in Tokyo, Japan, Miyazaki's cinematic journey spans decades and has left an indelible mark on the world of animation and filmmaking. As the co-founder of Studio Ghibli, a renowned animation studio in Japan, Miyazaki has crafted a mesmerizing array of films that captivate audiences of all ages, blending fantastical storytelling with profound philosophical insights.
